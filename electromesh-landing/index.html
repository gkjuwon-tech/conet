— 01 / how it works

sixteen handshakes,
one mental model.

the agent picks the right pairing flow for every class — pin for the fridge, qr for your phone, docker for the nas, captive-portal for the console. then attests with a sha256 pow and starts heartbeating.

  1. 01
    claim your lan with a one-time email code
    otp-locked. we refuse to let anyone in a starbucks pair your phone to their account.
  2. 02
    pick a device — sixteen classes, one click each
    phone? qr. fridge? 6-digit pin. router? paste one curl|sh line.
  3. 03
    idle = earning
    phones run a service worker that wakes every push, mines for ≤22 s, and sleeps. bulbs only mine while the lamp is off.
  4. 04
    withdraw — never run at a loss
    the economics engine refuses to dispatch work below your electricity floor + 30% safety. earnings beat power. always.

— 06 / faq

yes, we get that question all the time.

will my phone overheat?

no. the throttle backs off above 78 °C and pauses entirely above 88 °C. phones are also pinned to charging-only mode by default.

how do you stop someone in a starbucks pairing my phone?

lan ownership requires an email otp linked to a 24-hour-old account and a 24-hour grace window real owners can dispute through. max 5 verified lan claims per user.

does the phone have to stay open?

no. add the pwa to your home screen and the service worker takes over. web push wakes it up; periodic background sync handles quiet windows; a silent audio loop keeps backgrounded tabs alive.

when do i get paid?

wallet earns continuously. withdrawals via stripe go out the same day for balances ≥ $1.00.
